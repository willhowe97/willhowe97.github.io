Registration

In order to register for the app, all you need to do is input the first part of your postcode on any smartphone device.

Simple Symptom Reporting

If you are displaying COIVD-19 symptoms, or have received a positive test, then you simply have to press the "Report a positive result" button.

Location Data Donation

After reporting symptoms, exposed contacts will be notified and your stored location data will be uploaded. This is to be used to assist in tracking the spread of the disease.
