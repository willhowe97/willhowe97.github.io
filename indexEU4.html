Registration

In order to register for the app, all you need to do is input your postcode on any smartphone device.

Bluetooth Broadcast

Once enabled, your contact tracing app will regularly send out Bluetooth signals we call "beacons". This broadcasts a random number that is not tied to your identity and changes every 10-20 minutes.

Other phones will be listening for these broadcasted signals whilst broadcasting their own. Recevied beacons are recorded and securely stored on the device.

Simple Symptom Reporting

If you are displaying COIVD-19 symptoms, or have received a positive test, then you simply have to press the "Report a positive result" button.

No Additional Data Required

After reporting symptoms, the last 14 days of keys for your Bluetooth beacons will be uploaded to the server. Each phone periodically downloads the Bluetooth beacon keys of everyone who has tested positive for COVID-19. This allows it to determine if you have been exposed to a potential infection risk.
